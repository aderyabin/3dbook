• The Orange Book, formally titled Approved Drug Products With Therapeutic Equivalence Evaluations, is a comprehensive list of approved drug products published by the FDA

    It is widely accepted as the authoritative source for determining therapeutic equivalence among multisource drug products. Available on-line as the Electronic Orange Book (EOB), it aims to provide public information and advice to health professionals and agencies about drug product selection and to foster cost containment. Therapeutic equivalence (TE) ratings are independent of approval status.
